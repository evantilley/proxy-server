Privacy Policy


We like to think our privacy policy is pretty simple. We don't collect any data, period. We do monitor overall app usage metadata statistics (such as total online active users, posts created, etc.), but we don't save this data and/or analyze it. We simply check in from time to time to gain a sense of how our app is resonating with users. One important thing to note: We use Firebase, a service provided by Google, to help store data on our back end. You can check out their privacy policy here. Our privacy policy is subject to change and we will let users know if this does happen.
